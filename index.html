Privacy Policy for DartStats

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DartStats ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application. By using DartStats, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with a functional dart tracking experience:

  • Account Information: When you register or log in, we collect your email address and username through Firebase Authentication. This is required for account management and identifying your data across devices.
  • Game Statistics: We store your dart scores, game history (e.g., 501, 121), and double attempts in Google Firebase/Firestore. This data is used solely to provide your personal performance statistics.
  • Device Information: We may automatically collect certain device information (OS version, model, and unique identifiers) to help diagnose bugs and improve app stability.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase (a service provided by Google LLC) for our backend infrastructure. Firebase handles the following on our behalf:

  • Authentication: Securely managing your login credentials.
  • Firestore Database: Storing your game statistics.
  • Crashlytics: Monitoring app performance and crashes.

Data processed by Firebase is encrypted in transit and at rest. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy and Security.

3. Data Usage and Sharing

Your data is used strictly for provide the App's features. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. To request deletion, please contact us at the email below or use the account management features within the App.

5. Security

We value your trust and use industry-standard security measures provided by Google Firebase to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at our GitHub Repository.
