Overview

DartStats is a powerful, data-driven Android application designed to help darts players of all levels track their progress, analyze their game, and compete with friends. Built with modern Android technologies, it combines offline practice tools with real-time online PvP.

Whether you're practicing your doubles, running a structured training routine, or challenging a friend across the globe, DartStats handles the math so you can focus on the throw.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 3, 2026

This policy describes how DartStats ("we," "our," or "the App") handles your information. By using DartStats, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email and username (via Firebase Authentication) for account management.
  • Game Data: Dart scores and history (stored in Firestore) to provide personal statistics.
  • Device Data: OS version and identifiers for crash reporting and diagnostics.

2. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication, database, and crash monitoring. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
