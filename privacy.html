Privacy Policy for DartStats

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

DartStats provides dart game tracking and statistics. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your email address and a username to facilitate the Login/Logout and sync functionality. Game statistics (scores, double attempts) are stored to provide you with performance history.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the DartStats experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transit.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us via our GitHub repository.
